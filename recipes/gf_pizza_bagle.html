Gluten Free Pizza Bagles

Description

Pizza bagles are a family favorite. They are easy to make after a long day. I prefer to use gluten free bagles because they don't get as soggy as regular bagles.

Ingredients

  • Gluten Free Bagles
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Package of pre-sliced peperoni (optional)
  • Aluminum Foil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Cover pans with foil (Optional)
  3. Cut bagles in half
  4. Cover bagles with marinara sauce
  5. Top with shredded cheese
  6. Top with peperoni (optional)
  7. Bake until cheese is melted
